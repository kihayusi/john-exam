Sinigang na Baboy

Classic Filipino sour pork soup with vegetables

‚è±Ô∏è Prep: 15 mins
üî• Cook: 45 mins
üçΩÔ∏è Serves: 6
‚≠ê Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly or pork ribs, cut into pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 cup long green beans (sitaw), cut into 3-inch pieces
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2-3 pieces green chili (siling haba)
  • 1 packet (or 1/2 cup) tamarind mix or fresh tamarind for sourness
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine pork and water. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30-40 minutes until pork is tender. Skim off scum as needed.
  2. Add onion and tomatoes. Continue simmering for 10 minutes to release flavor.
  3. Add radish and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add eggplant, long beans, and green chili. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add tamarind mix or fresh tamarind juice. Stir and adjust sourness according to taste.
  6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  7. Finally, add kangkong leaves. Cook for 1-2 minutes until wilted.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.

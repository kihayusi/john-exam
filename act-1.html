Pancit Bihon

Classic Filipino stir-fried rice noodles

‚è±Ô∏è Prep: 15 mins
üî• Cook: 15 mins
üçΩÔ∏è Serves: 6
‚≠ê Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) bihon rice noodles
  • 1/2 lb (225g) chicken breast, sliced thinly
  • 1/4 lb (115g) pork, sliced thinly
  • 8 pieces medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges for serving
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Soak the bihon noodles in water for 10 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large wok or pan over medium-high heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add the pork and chicken. Cook until the meat is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Bring to a boil.
  6. Add the carrots and green beans. Cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add the soaked bihon noodles. Toss everything together, making sure the noodles absorb the liquid.
  8. Add the cabbage and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the noodles are tender and the vegetables are cooked but still crisp.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Transfer to a serving platter and garnish with green onions. Serve with calamansi or lemon wedges.

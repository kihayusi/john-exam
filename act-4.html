Filipino Sweet Spaghetti

A kid-favorite Filipino spaghetti with sweet sauce and hotdog slices

‚è±Ô∏è Prep: 10 mins
üî• Cook: 20 mins
üçΩÔ∏è Serves: 6
‚≠ê Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 500g spaghetti noodles
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 300g ground pork
  • 200g hotdogs, sliced diagonally
  • 1 can (250g) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup banana ketchup
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for topping

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add ground pork and cook until browned.
  4. Add sliced hotdogs and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce, banana ketchup, and condensed milk. Stir well and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Season with soy sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper. Adjust sweetness as preferred.
  7. Add the cooked spaghetti noodles and toss until evenly coated with the sauce.
  8. Serve hot and sprinkle generously with grated cheese.
